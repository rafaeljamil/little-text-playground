Little Text Playground

How it works:

Type or paste some text in the first text area.

Choose an option according to what you want to happen to your text.

Hit "Transform".

Now simply copy the resulting text and paste it wherever you want.

Have fun!

Options available:

  • lowercase - changes the whole text to lowercase
  • UPPERCASE - changes the whole text to uppercase
  • Random - scramble the word placement of a given phrase (does not work with single words)
  • EndeS EsseS - add "s" or "es" to the end of each given word
  • CaMeLcAsE - applies lowercase and uppercase for every other letter
  • S p a c e d - adds spaces between every character
  • Mirror - inverts the text order, from last to first
